Rate of Sale Calculator – Assortment Planning ROS Scenario Results

Once scenarios have been calculated, users can review the resulting rates of sale by defined performance bands (set during configuration) as both raw and adjusted (cleansed) values.  The results are grouped into the following performance percentiles (configurable during implementation):

  • Minimum
  • 25th percentile
  • Median (50th percentile)
  • 75th percentile
  • Maximum
  • Average (default selection)

Average shows the true average rate of sale per variant/period for the selected data, not the average from the configured percentile bands

  1. Review Raw ROS and Adjusted ROS sales distributions
    1. The sales history for the selected items is distributed across a performance percentile  (Worst, 30%, 50%, 70%, Best) (Range is configurable during design)
    2. Raw ROS = No data cleansing or filtering
    3. Adjusted ROS = Sales history that has been cleansed by the data cleansing steps outlined previously in this document
    4. Record Count = the number of data points (style/color sales periods) used in the calculation of the displayed rates of sale – to serve as an indication of relative data strength
    5. Effective ROS = Is the ROS that will be applied to the list of placeholders/variants in the third and bottom view of the ROS calculator screen.  The Effective ROS can be adjusted by checking the ROS from the Scenario results, or by manually entering a value.  ROS selection is cluster specific and the distribution selection can vary by cluster
