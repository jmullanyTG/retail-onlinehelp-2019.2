Cell Lock Example

The simplest locking scenario is when we lock a single period of sales to keep its sales values intact when changes are made at aggregate levels above it

In the above example, Sales has been locked for 2017_wk32, also locking Sales Units and AUR in the view

When Sales is changed to $25,000 at the month level, the system subtracts the locked quantity of $5,044 from $25,000, and distributes the remaining $19,956 across the 3 unlocked periods by taking their percent contribution to the old Sales total, rebalancing them to equal 100%, then multiplying by $19,956
