Merchandise Financial Planning

The following enhancements are now available in the Financial Planning solution.

FX Neutralized LY metrics DMDEV-62078

FX Neutralized LY metrics have been added to Hybrid plan types. These metrics allow businesses to plan by referencing comparable LY/LLY data using current exchange rates applied to both TY and LY/LLY values.

Adjusting AUC and AUC % +/- LY DMDEV-59296

As of v2019.2, you can plan your business by adjusting AUC and AUC % +/- LY and having the related sales metrics calculation based on that change.
