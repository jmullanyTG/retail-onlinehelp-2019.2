Assortment Planning

New columns in the Manage Locations screen DMDEV-61527

To assist in ensuring the right decision is arrived at, the ROS and Adj ROS columns have been removed from the Manage Locations screen and replaced by new Sales columns.

Removed columns New columns
ROS $ Total Sales ($)
ROS U Total Sales (U)
Adj ROS $ Adjusted Sales ($)
Adj ROS U Adjusted Sales (U)

New additional sales by price type metrics for comparison and carryover data

There are new additional sales by type metrics in the Target Creation worksheet for comparison data and carryover data. For example,

  • Comparison Reg Sales $
  • Comparison Reg Sales U
  • Comparison Reg AUR
  • Comparison Reg Productivity
  • TY C/O Reg Sales $
  • TY C/O Reg Sales U
  • TY C/O Reg AUR
  • TY C/O Reg Productivity
