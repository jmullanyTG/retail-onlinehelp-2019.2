Graph Viewer

The graph viewer will display the different series related to the forecast in a graphical format. Users can configure the colors of the various available series within the graph selecting a color palette which is most appealing and accessible for them.

NOTE: The ability to configure a series’ color requires user right Allow Series Configuration.

Hovering the mouse over the graph will pop up the infobox, a detailed description of the values for the time period.

Within the graph viewer, users may manipulate the red dot series (Manual Forecast) in order to affect a manual forecast value.
